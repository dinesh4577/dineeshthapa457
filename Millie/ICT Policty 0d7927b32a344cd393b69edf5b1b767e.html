ICT Policty

  • What do you mean by IT and IT policy?

    Information Technology is the modern technology of computer science, which includes fast communication from on place of world to another place

    The policy formulated by the government or concerned authority to organize, develop and implement in different sectors of IT systematically, is known as IT policy.

  • What are the objectives of IT policy 2072 of Nepal?

    The objectives of IT policy 2072 of Nepal are as follows:

    1. To make ICT accessible and affordable for all.
    1. To promote good governance with the use of ICT.
    1. To develop and extend the infrastructure of ICT.
    1. It acheive sustainable, wide and inclusive socio-economic development with the use of ICT.
  • Write down the guiding principle of ICT policy 2072

    The guiding principle of ICT policy 2072 are:

    1. ICT will be the powerful medium into change Nepal as a knowledge-based society
    1. Universal public access will be ensured for the basic ICT infrastructure.
    1. Technology neutrality will be ensured in ICT services.
    1. Concept of infrastructure synergy will be adopted.
  • What are the goals of ICT policy 2072?

    The goals of ICT policy 2072 are:

    1. At least 75 per cent of population will have digital literacy skills by the end of 2020.
    1. All the government broadcasting sectors will be transformed to digital form by 2017.
    1. By 2020, entire population of Nepal would have access to internet.
    1. Contribution of ICT industries and services ( excluding telecom) will increase in the Nepali economy accounting for at least 2.5 % percent of GDP by 2020.
  • What is e-governance

    E-Governance is defined as the application of electronic means in the interaction between government and citizens and government and business, to simplify and improve democratic, government and business, to simplify and improve democratic, government and business aspects of governance.
